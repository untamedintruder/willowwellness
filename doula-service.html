Doula Services We Offer

  • Prenatal Doula Services: Comprehensive support during pregnancy, including emotional guidance, birth planning, and education on birthing options, comfort measures, and postpartum recovery.
  • Birth Doula Services: Continuous support during labor and delivery, including assistance with pain management techniques, emotional reassurance, physical comfort, and advocating for the birthing person's wishes with the healthcare team.
  • Postpartum Doula Services: Assistance during the postpartum period, offering emotional support, breastfeeding assistance, newborn care guidance, and light household help to allow families to adjust to their new routine.
  • Sibling and Family Support: Helping families adjust to the arrival of a new baby by providing support for older siblings and the entire family during the transition.
  • Bereavement Doula Services: Compassionate support for families who experience a loss, offering emotional care, guidance, and assistance during the grieving process.
